24 Hour Film Challenge

Writer/Director/Colorist

Upon receiving a prompt for the script, the entire film was written, produced, shot, editing, and colored in under 24 hours. Following an intense but brief writing session, the production team scrambled to get everything needed to turn the script into hard results. Despite time constraints (and lack of sleep), we were able to pull everything together into a dark comedy.
